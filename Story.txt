**story**
Once upon a time,in a forest, there lived a monkey who recieded in a Jamun tree, which was on the banks of a river.
In the same forest there lived the crocodile and his wife. One day the crocodile came to the banks of the river and
 rested under the tree. The kind hearted monkey offered him some fruits, the crocodile came back th enext day for some
 more  fruits as he loved them. As the days passed by the crocodile and the monkey became good friends.
